What is Voluntr

A mobile platform that connects students with local volunteering and internship opportunities through systems they are used to — all in one simple, social feed.

Why Voluntr exists

Finding meaningful opportunities shouldn't require hours of searching. Voluntr makes discovery effortless so students can spend less time looking and more time doing.

Who Voluntr helps

Students ready to make an impact, and organizations ready to welcome them.
