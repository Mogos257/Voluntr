Our Story

Voluntr started with a simple frustration — as students ourselves, we knew how hard it was to find volunteering and internship opportunities that were actually worth our time. The information was scattered, the processes were outdated, and most platforms weren't built with students in mind.

So we decided to build something better. What started as a class project idea quickly grew into something we genuinely believed in. We wanted to create a space where students could discover opportunities the same way they discover everything else — quickly, visually, and on their phones.

We're three high school students who care about community, and we built Voluntr because we want the next generation of volunteers and interns to spend less time searching and more time actually showing up. This is just the beginning, and we're excited to grow it alongside the organizations and students who need it most.
