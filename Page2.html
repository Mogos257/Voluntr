Bread Of Life

Bread Of Life is a local organization based in Malden that raises and passes out food for those in need in the surrounding areas. They are looking for volunteers interested in helping on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays to stock up the food pantry with food coming from delivery trucks. This entails medium physical labor for around 2-3 hours.
